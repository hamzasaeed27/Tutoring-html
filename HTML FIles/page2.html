Elementary School Programs (Grades: 1-5)

ViBrainium after school program applauds young students and parents for taking initiative in thinking about the future.

ViBrainium aims to provide a safe space for students to learn and grow within their community.

ViBrainium offers many courses for our elementary level students. We offer courses in math, science, and arts. Our teachers are trained professionals and give care to each and every child as if they were their own. We here at ViBrainium cherish our youth and hope to push them toward a better future. We value honesty, responsibility, respect, work ethic, and kindness.

ACTIVITIES THAT OUR STUDENTS ARE OFFERED.....

  1. Math excercises to strengthen core mathematical skills
  2. Hands on science labs to teach children about real world scientific application
  3. Elementary level painting courses for children to express themselves at their own pace
  4. Productive one on one time for students who are struggling in their respective courses
  5. Social activities to build student character and strengthen social skills.
